
## Overview
Every run ends one of four ways. Each has its own 
screen. All are valid. None are identical.
The ending is part of the story of that run.

## Death
Shot during a job, ambush, showdown, or bounty hunter 
encounter. The most common ending.
- Screen: Wanted poster. Name, cause of death, 
  total haul, days survived, bounty at time of death.
- Music: slow, mournful

## Prison
Caught alive. Could be a failed escape, surrounded 
with no ammo, or surrendered.
- Screen: Prison cell. Your poster on the wall.
- Future: prison escape mechanic possible
- Music: quiet, defeated

## Gone to Texas (GTT)
The prestige ending. Reach the border exit node 
with enough money and reputation to disappear.
Rarest ending. Most satisfying.
- Screen: Cinematic ride to the border. 
  Sun setting. Your crew riding alongside.
  Fade to black. Your legend written out.
- Requirements: reach final region exit + 
  money threshold TBD
- Music: triumphant, earned

## Bounty Hunter Got You
Special death. A bounty hunter catches up and wins.
Different from regular death — he takes you in or 
puts you down depending on your wanted status.
- Dead or Alive distinction matters here
- Screen: TBD — his poster next to yours
