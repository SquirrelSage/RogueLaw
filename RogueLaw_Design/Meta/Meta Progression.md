
## Overview
Meta progression exists to reward continued play without 
making the game easier in a boring way. Unlocks add 
variety, tension, and replayability — not just power.
Every unlock should make a run feel different, 
not just easier.

## Meta Currency
**Legend Points** — earned every run based on:
- Total haul
- Bounty at time of death
- Days survived
- Endings achieved (GTT pays most)
- Achievements triggered

Spent at the Cottonwood Sign between runs.

## The Cottonwood Sign
The main meta hub. A weathered signpost outside of town.
- Lists all your past characters, their bounty, 
  their ending, their legend
- Where you spend Legend Points
- Where you place tombstones for dead characters
- Your highest bounty is carved into the wood
- As it fills up it becomes a piece of art — 
  your personal history of runs

## Ghosts
One of the coolest meta concepts.

Previous run characters come back as ghosts.
They haunt locations you visit during new runs.
You might see your old character standing outside 
a saloon you robbed three runs ago.

### Putting Them to Rest
- Find a graveyard location
- Place a tombstone for your dead character
- Tombstones cost Legend Points
- Once placed, that ghost is at peace
- Their name is carved permanently on the stone

### Ghost Effects (if not put to rest)
- They appear as omens — bad luck events trigger nearby
- They can help you — pointing toward loot or danger
- Higher bounty ghosts are more powerful / more restless
- Too many unrest ghosts = supernatural events escalate

### Supernatural Element
God intervenes. Rare divine or cursed events mid-run.
Could be punishment for too many kills.
Could be reward for a clean run.
TBD — keep it rare, keep it weird, keep it western.
