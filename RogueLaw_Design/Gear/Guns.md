
## Sidearms
- Iron — generic term for any pistol
- Six-Shooter — standard revolver. Reliable, common.
- Peacemaker — Colt Single Action Army. The iconic cowboy gun.
- Derringer — tiny concealable pistol. Low damage, last resort or surprise.

## Long Guns
- Repeater — lever action rifle. Good rate of fire.
- Winchester — premium repeater. The Repeater but better.
- Buckshot — shotgun shell type. Close range devastation.
