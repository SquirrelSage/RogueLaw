
Every town has an archetype that determines its layout, 
buildings, NPC types, and name pool. Same system, 
different soul.

## Cattle Town
The classic Western town. Ranchers, cowboys, dusty streets.
- Buildings: Saloon, General Store, Stable, Bank (possible), Sheriff (likely)
- Vibe: Busy, lawful, familiar
- Name pool: Red, Dry, Dusty, Dead, Iron + Creek, Gulch, Ridge, Flats

## Mining Camp
Built around a strike. Could boom or bust any day.
- Buildings: Saloon, Assay Office, General Store, Gunsmith (likely), Bank (possible)
- Vibe: Rough, transient, desperate
- Name pool: Gold, Copper, Iron, Ashen + Vein, Shaft, Hollow, Claim

## River Crossing
A town that exists because of water. Ferries, traders, travelers.
- Buildings: Saloon, Stable, Ferry Office, General Store, Train Station (possible)
- Vibe: Transient, neutral, trade-focused
- Name pool: Muddy, Swift, Still, Broken + Ford, Crossing, Bend, Landing

## Boom Town
Money is here right now. Banks, buyers, and trouble.
- Buildings: Saloon, Bank (always), General Store, Gunsmith, Stable
- Vibe: Loud, crowded, high heat
- Name pool: New, Fort, Grand, High + Hope, Chance, Strike, Fortune
