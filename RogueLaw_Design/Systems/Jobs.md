
## Overview
Jobs are the primary way to progress. They are almost 
always illegal, always risky, and always consequential.
Jobs get you money, gear, horses, upgrades, and skills.
They are where your crew, traits, and gear all come 
together. Lots to play with here.

## Job Outcomes
Jobs can go multiple ways depending on execution:
- **Clean** — no witnesses, no heat, full payout
- **Quiet** — minimal noise, low heat, full payout
- **Loud** — guards alerted, high heat, full payout
- **Deadly** — kills involved, bounty increase, 
  wanted status affected
- **Failed** — partial heat, crew casualties, no payout

## Wanted Status
Determined by kill count across the run:
- **Wanted Alive** — few or no kills. Law wants you 
  breathing. Bounty hunters try to capture.
- **Wanted Dead or Alive** — middle ground. Most common.
- **Wanted Dead** — too many kills. Shoot on sight. 
  No capture attempts. Bounty hunters go lethal.

## Job Rewards
- Money
- Gear and weapons
- Horses
- Upgrades and skills
- Intel on other jobs

## Job Planning
Jobs are planned before execution. Crew roles matter:
- Safecracker for banks
- Lookout reduces heat
- Muscle increases success chance
- Sharpshooter for deadly jobs
- Doctor keeps crew alive

## Job Resolution
Top-down combat scene. Player + crew vs guards.
Objective varies by job type. Escape is always 
the final goal.
