
## Overview
Bounty is personal infamy. Every crew member has their 
own bounty, including you. Bounty is not a punishment —
it is your legend. The higher your bounty, the more 
the world knows your name.

## How Bounty Is Gained
- NPC knowledge of your deeds — witnesses, survivors, 
  loose tongues
- Completing jobs — bigger jobs, bigger bounty
- Killing lawmen
- Bounty grows naturally as the run progresses

## Bounty Is Good
- Higher bounty = more aura
- Unlocks additional crew slots
- Outlaws approach YOU and ask to ride with you
- Meta game — highest bounty in the west leaderboard
- Endgame goal — build the biggest legend possible
- Achievements tied to bounty thresholds

## How Bounty Is Known
- Wanted posters in towns — your face, your number
- NPCs react differently at different bounty levels
- You never see your exact number directly — 
  you learn it through the world

## Bounty Hunters
At a certain threshold a bounty hunter spawns.
- Uses heat to pathfind toward you
- Higher bounty = harder bounty hunter
- If you kill or shake one, a stronger one is sent
- These guys are meant to end the game
- Almost unkillable at high bounty levels
- Each hunter is named, has traits, has a reputation

## Crew Bounty
Each crew member has their own bounty.
- Crew with high bounty are harder to replace if lost
- High bounty crew attract attention independently
