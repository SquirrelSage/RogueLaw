
## Overview
Heat applies to locations, not the player directly. 
Every town and location tracks its own heat level.
High heat makes a location dangerous. Max heat burns 
a town — it is gone forever for that run.

## How Heat Is Gained
- Completing jobs (successful or failed)
- Failed jobs generate partial heat
- Talking — saying the wrong thing to the wrong person
- Bad deeds in town (stealing, fighting, killing)
- Being recognized (high bounty in a high-heat town)

## What Heat Does (Escalating)
- **Low** — NPCs react to your name. Wanted posters appear.
- **Mid** — Buildings close off. Prices spike. 
  Sheriff encounters more likely. Job security increases.
- **High** — Most buildings inaccessible. Law actively 
  patrols. Jobs have maximum guards.
- **Max** — Town BURNS. Cannot return for the rest of 
  the run. Gone forever.

## Heat & Bounty Hunters
Bounty hunters use heat as a pathfinding tool. They 
move toward high-heat locations looking for you. 
Like an optimized search algorithm — they go where 
the crime is, because that's where you are.
