
## Overview
Every run you play as a new randomly generated outlaw.
No two runs start the same. Your starting stats and 
traits define your build — you play around them, 
not against them.

## Introduction Sequence
Game menu opens on a train. Click play — zoom inside.
Your character sits riding. His name, age, and stats 
appear on screen. Cinematic, still, aura.
Then the train stops. The run begins.

## Traits (2 per run, randomly rolled)
Traits are large modifiers. +/- % on core stats.
They define your build before you make a single decision.

### Example Traits
| Trait | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| Deadeye | +25% accuracy, -10% speed |
| Trueshot | +20% long range damage |
| Two Left Hands | -20% accuracy, +10% luck |
| Drunk | -15% accuracy, +20% charm, unpredictable events |
| Night Rider | +30% stealth at night, -20% day performance |
| Silver Tongue | +25% charm, -10% strength |
| Iron Stomach | Immune to poison and bad food |
| Hotheaded | +15% damage, more likely to trigger fights |
| Coward | -20% combat, +30% escape chance |
| Lucky | Random positive events more likely |
