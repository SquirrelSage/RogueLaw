
## Overview
Crew members are hired outlaws who ride with you. 
They have their own health, stats, equippables, and 
personality. Planning your crew is planning your run.

Solo runs are possible and rewarded with achievements.
Crew makes the game easier but also more expensive, 
more complicated, and higher stakes.

## Hiring Types
- **Flat Fee** — one job, paid upfront. No loyalty built.
- **Cut of Booty** — permanent hire, takes a percentage 
  of every payout. Loyal crew.
- **Random Join** — high bounty attracts outlaws who 
  approach YOU. Increases with bounty level.

## Crew Slots
- Start with 2 slots
- More slots unlock as bounty increases
- Each crew member needs their own horse (you buy it)

## Classes
- Muscle — brute force, success chance
- Sharpshooter — combat accuracy, damage
- Safecracker — bank payout bonus
- Lookout — reduces heat after jobs
- Doctor — heals crew in the field
- Coosie — camp cook. Unique class. Restores stamina 
  and morale on long rides. Special trait only they have.

## Loyalty (Hidden Stat)
Player never sees the loyalty number directly. It affects:
- Whether they stay after getting wounded
- Chance of betrayal
- Likelihood of going permanent after a flat fee job
- Flavor text during raids and fights

## Crew Death
Crew members can die on failed jobs. Losing them hurts. 
They are not easy to replace.
